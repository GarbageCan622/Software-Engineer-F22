Project desription

On our first meeting, everyone on the team complained about how BlackBoard displays grades. They are spread out for each class, rarely is a percentage or letter grade present for your current grade, and there is no way to see how it may impact your GPA. So as a team we agreed to create an enhanced gradebook that theoretically would replace the current gradebook in BlackBoard. In addition to the problems present above, we have ideas for adding new features to the gradebook, like advanced sorting and entering mock grades to see how an upcoming assignment may impact your course grade. Overall, we hope to create a user-friendly gradebook that will be a useful tool for students and professors alike.
